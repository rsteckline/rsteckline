### Bobby Steckline ◦ Software Engineer

<b>I'm a software engineer with a background in support and over 10 years of experience in team management and customer-facing roles. I focus on building accessible, user-friendly interfaces using JavaScript, React, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, and Tailwind. I'm comfortable working across Agile teams and enjoy balancing clean UI design with strong, test-driven development. Whether collaborating or leading, I bring a mix of technical skills and real-world problem-solving to every project.</b>
<br>

<b>Currently, I’m part of a healthcare tech startup where I help build a HIPAA-compliant platform for remote patient monitoring and virtual doctor visits. Working across a full-stack environment with Ruby on Rails and React, I develop features that improve how clinicians manage patient data, including generating secure exports of protected health information, customizing alerts, and streamlining care plan workflows. I work closely with both technical and clinical stakeholders to ensure the platform meets real-world needs. I contribute to both the backend logic and the front-end UI, with a focus on security, performance, and usability in a high-stakes clinical setting.<b>
